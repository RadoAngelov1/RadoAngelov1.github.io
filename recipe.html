Recipe #1 Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1 Cup
Sugar 1 Cup
Brown Sugar 1 Cup
Eggs 2
Sugar 1 Cup
Vanilla Extract 2 Teaspoons
Baking Soda 1 Teaspoon
Hot Water 2 Teaspoons
Salt 1/2 Teaspoon
Flour 3 Cups
Chocolate Chips 2 Cups

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Step 2: Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
Step 3: Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
Step 4: Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
Step 5: Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
Step 6: Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

Recipe #2 Spaghetti Cacio e Pepe

  • Time: 23 minutes
  • Serves: 4 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 1/2 kg
Olive Oil 6 Tablespoons
Garlic 2 Cloves
Ground Black Pepper 2 Teaspoons
Pecorino Romano Cheese 1 and 3/4 Cup

Step 1:Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
Step2: ook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally for about about 10 minutes.
Step 3: Scoop out some of the cooking water and reserve. Drain spaghetti.
Step 4: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
Step 5: Add garlic and pepper; cook and stir until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
Step 6: Add spaghetti and Pecorino Romano cheese. Ladle in 1/2 cup of reserved cooking water; stir until cheese is melted, about 1 minute.
Step 7: Add more cooking water until sauce coats spaghetti, about 1 minute more.

Recipe #3 Banana Bread

  • Time: 1h 20m
  • Serves: 12 servings
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 Cups
Baking Soda 1 Teaspoon
Salt 1/4 Teaspoon
Butter 1/2 Cup
Brown Sugar 3/4 Cup
Eggs 2
Mashed Bananas 2 and 1/3 Cups

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Step 2: In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt.
Step 3:In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar.
Step 4: Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
Step 5: Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten.
Step 6: Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
Step 7: Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.
